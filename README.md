# Mandeta Ads

Mandeta Ads is a programmatic advertising platform purpose-built 
for Albanian-speaking markets — the first of its kind.

---

## The Platform

Mandeta Ads combines a full ad tech stack under one roof:

- **DSP** — advertisers buy inventory in real time with precision 
  targeting across Albanian digital media
- **SSP** — publishers monetize inventory through a unified 
  commercial and technical framework
- **DMP** — audience data is collected, organized, and activated 
  for sharper targeting and better campaign performance
- **Ad Server** — powered by Mandeta's own creative platform, 
  delivering live ad previews and real-time campaign control

## Campaign Objectives

Mandeta Ads supports the full funnel:

- **Awareness** — maximum impressions, brand visibility across 
  premium Albanian publishers
- **Traffic** — drive clicks to your website or landing page
- **Lead generation** — capture high-intent users from a 
  hard-to-reach demographic
- **Retargeting** — re-engage users who already visited your site

---

## How It Works

**For advertisers:**
1. Choose your format — Core, Wow, or both
2. Set your base budget and retargeting budget
3. Select your campaign objective and targeting parameters
4. Go self-serve via the dashboard, or hand it to our managed 
   service team
5. Monitor performance in real time through your reporting dashboard

**For publishers:**
1. Join the Mandeta publisher network
2. Connect your inventory to the SSP
3. Access programmatic demand from local and global advertisers
4. Monetize with transparent, real-time reporting
